2021.04.10 | blair

Pretty immediately after opening my Ender I got overwhelmed as I spread the parts over my limited aparment work area, so I watched a parts tour & assembly video to get a sense of what I was in for. The video linked says the instructions included in the box are straightforward if you're "mechanically minded"; this may well be true, but I knew that other videos would likely have tips & tricks to ensure better initial assembly. I ended up settling on this video, based mostly off view count/likes. I watched it through once on 2x speed to get a sense of scale, then started from the beginning.
